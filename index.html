About Me

Hi my name is Ian Dunkelberg. I am a full-stack web developer. I recently graduated from Western Washington University with a degree in Computer Science and Mathematics in June of 2020 and I’m deeply familiar with modern web technologies.

I love finding solutions. At Western Washington University I focused on using Mathematics to find computer based solutions to complex mathematical problems. During this process I realized that I could solve even more ambitious problems by applying my skills to the web. I graduated from the University of Washington’s Full-Stack Web Development bootcamp in February of 2019.

My experience in the bootcamp gave me the tools necessary to craft elegant solutions to modern problems. For our capstone project, my team of three created an in browser front end web development environment using the MERN stack which you can try out here.
